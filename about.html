Hi, I’m Amanda You

I am currently a student at Fresno City College majoring in Computer Information Systems and Web Developer. I am a mother of 3 and married to my husband of 15 years. We reside in Fresno, CA and bought our first home here in 2019. I enjoy home renovating/decorating, trying to keep my plants alive indoor and outdoor, and making memories with my family. We love going to the movies, cooking together, being outdoor and our favorite is taking trips to the beach. I'm hoping to do new things like camping being our next family adventure!

I'm originally from southern California but I love living in Fresno, CA because of the slow pace life and community of our city. I am a Cali girl and always will be but hopefully one day be able to move somewhere where I can enjoy all, well some of the four seasons. Only in the near future will tell where that will be.
