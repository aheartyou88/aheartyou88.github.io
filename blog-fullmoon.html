  • 5 October, 2023

    • These are the phases of the moon for this month of October. These are things to look for:

    It is said we will be able to see partial of the solar eclipse on October 14th. Around noon ET the moon will begin to pass in front of the sun blocking 42% at its peak by 1:19pm, ending at 2:39pm.

    On Saturday afternoon, October 28th at 4:24pm ET we will see the Full Moon

    These four moons are said to be "Supermoons" the biggest and brighterst full Moons for the year.
